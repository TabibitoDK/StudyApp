Oxidising agent

(Undergo reduction)

Half equation

Acidified potassium manganate (VII), KMnO4

MnO4- (aq) + 8H+ (aq) + 5e- à Mn2+ (aq) + 4H2O (l)

Acidified potassium dichromate (VI), K2Cr2O7

Cr2O72- (aq) + 14H+ (aq) + 6e- à 2Cr3+ (aq) + 7H2O (l)

Chloride water, Cl2

Cl2 (aq) + 2e- à 2Cl- (aq)

Bromine water, Br2

Br2 (aq) + 2e- à 2Br- (aq)

Iron (III) chloride, FeCl3

Fe3+ (aq) + e- à Fe2+ (aq)

Acidified hydrogen peroxide, H2O2

H2O2 (aq) + 2H+ (aq) + 2e- à 2H2O (aq)
