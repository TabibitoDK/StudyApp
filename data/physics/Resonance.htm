Damping is the decrease in amplitude of an oscillating system

Due to loses of energy to the surrounding as heat energy

Frequency unchanged

Oscillation undergoes damping = damped oscillation

 Types of damping

External damping

Internal damping

Oscillating system loses energy to overcome friction or air resistance.

Oscillating system loses energy because of the stretching and compression of the vibrating particles in the system.

 

Force oscillation

The effect of damping / damped oscillation can be overcome by applying periodic external force on the oscillating system

The periodic external force transfers energy into the oscillating system to replace the energy lost

The system is said to be in a forced oscillation.

Force oscillation

Oscillations with the help of external force / forces

 

Periodic force

force which acts at specific time intervals.

does not act continuously.
