Snell’s law

Determining refractive angle or incidence angle of mediums

The incident ray, refracted ray and normal meet at one point and are in the same plane. è point of incidence

n1 sin θ1 = n2 sin θ2

 

n1 = refractive index of medium 1

n2 = refractive index of medium 2

θ1 = angle of incidence in medium 1

θ2 = angle of refraction in medium 2
